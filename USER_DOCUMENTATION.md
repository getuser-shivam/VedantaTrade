## Getting Started

### System Requirements
- **iOS**: iOS 12.0 or later
- **Android**: Android 5.0 (API level 21) or later
- **Internet Connection**: Required for most features
- **Storage**: At least 100MB free space

### Installation
1. **iOS**: Download from the App Store
2. **Android**: Download from Google Play Store
3. Search for "Neutralitical Health Supplements"
4. Tap "Install" and wait for download to complete

### First Launch
Upon first launch, you'll be greeted with a welcome screen. You can:
- Create a new account
- Log in with existing credentials
- Browse as a guest (limited features)

## Account Registration and Login

### Creating an Account
1. Tap "Sign Up" on the welcome screen
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Password requirements: Minimum 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, and numbers
4. Verify your email address via the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile with optional information

### Logging In
1. Tap "Log In" on the welcome screen
2. Enter your email and password
3. Enable "Remember Me" for automatic login on future app launches
4. Use "Forgot Password" if you've forgotten your credentials

## Checkout and Payment

### Secure Checkout Process
1. Review your order summary
2. Enter shipping and billing addresses
3. Select payment method (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
4. Apply discount codes if available
5. Confirm and place order

### Payment Security
- All payments are processed through secure gateways
- We do not store your payment information
- PCI DSS compliant payment processing

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
- **App won't load**: Check internet connection, restart app
- **Login problems**: Verify email/password, reset password if needed
- **Payment failed**: Check card details, try alternative payment method
- **Order not updating**: Wait a few minutes, refresh the page
