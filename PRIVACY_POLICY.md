# Privacy Policy for VedantaTrade

**Effective Date:** April 3, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 3, 2026

## Introduction

VedantaTrade ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our pharmaceutical distribution platform designed specifically for the Nepal market.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, username, password
- **Business Information:** Firm name, business address, city, registration numbers
- **Financial Information:** Transaction records, VAT numbers, payment details (processed securely)
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates for field force tracking (MR visits, delivery tracking)

### Usage Information
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App usage statistics and analytics
- Log data and error reports
- IP addresses and session information

### Healthcare-Specific Data
- Doctor visit records
- Sample distribution tracking
- Prescription-related data (where applicable)

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Platform Operations:** To provide pharmaceutical distribution services
2. **GPS Tracking:** Real-time field force location tracking for MR visits and deliveries
3. **Financial Compliance:** VAT calculation (13%) and IRDN-compliant reporting
4. **Inventory Management:** Stock tracking and order fulfillment
5. **Security:** Fraud prevention and account protection
6. **Communication:** Order updates, alerts, and system notifications

## Data Storage and Security

- **Location:** Data is stored on secure servers with Microsoft SQL Server
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest
- **Access Control:** Role-based access with 6-role permission system
- **Retention:** Data retained as per Nepal's data retention laws

## GPS Tracking and Location Data

Our platform requires GPS tracking for:
- **Medical Representatives:** Field visit verification with mandatory <50m accuracy
- **Delivery Tracking:** Real-time order dispatch and delivery confirmation
- **Territory Management:** Coverage analytics and route optimization

**Your Control:**
- GPS can be disabled, but core functionality may be limited
- Location history is accessible to authorized users only
- Background tracking requires explicit permission

## Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data with:
- **Authorized Personnel:** Within your organization based on role permissions
- **Service Providers:** Hosting, analytics, and security services
- **Legal Authorities:** When required by Nepali law or court order

## Your Rights (Nepal Data Protection)

You have the right to:
1. **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Correction:** Update inaccurate information
3. **Deletion:** Request account deletion (subject to legal retention requirements)
4. **Portability:** Export your data in standard formats
5. **Objection:** Opt-out of non-essential data processing

## Cookies and Tracking

We use:
- **Essential Cookies:** Session management, security
- **Analytics Cookies:** Usage patterns (anonymized)
- **Preference Cookies:** Language, display settings

## Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:
- **OpenStreetMap:** For map visualization (location data shared)
- **Payment Gateways:** For financial transactions
- **Push Notification Services:** For real-time alerts

## Children's Privacy

VedantaTrade is intended for business use by pharmaceutical professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18.
